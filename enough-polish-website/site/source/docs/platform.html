Non-MIDP Platforms

J2ME Polish can be adjusted to support several platforms that support more than the normal MIDP profile. The key for this support is the ${polish.home}/platforms.xml file, inclusion of platform specific (boot) classpaths, and the automatic invocation of preprocessors, postcompilers, finalizers and so on.

Following Platforms are supported out of the box:

  • BlackBerry
  • Palm
  • DoJa
